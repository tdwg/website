# The Time & Place Functional Subcommittee is looking for volunteers!

## About Time & Place
We help identify potential conference locations, evaluate venues, and work with prospective local hosts to support future TDWG meetings.
## Responsibilities
If you're interested in conference planning, have ideas for future locations, or simply want to help strengthen TDWG's global community, we'd love to have you join us.
## Get in touch
If you'd like to get involved or want more information, please email [tardis@tdwg.org](mailto:tardis@tdwg.org).

It's more fun when we do things together!
