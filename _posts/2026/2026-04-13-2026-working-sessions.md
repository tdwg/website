## What are the TDWG Working Sessions?

Interest, Task, and Maintenance Groups are the driving force for developing and maintaining TDWG standards, and participation is the essential factor that gives TDWG standards authority in open science. As the biodiversity informatics community grows every year, we hold online working sessions to:

- inform the wider biodiversity informatics community about the on-going work to develop and maintain standards;
- review progress to date;
- engage new contributors; and
- set the agenda for work.
